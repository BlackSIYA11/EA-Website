About Us 

EA ARTS is a black owned business that is ran by a female who saw an opportunity in society to start a business that would design and create different types of music glass plaques.
Through her love for art she saw an opportunity for her business to grow and deided to venture into designing clothes and shoes for people.Our mission as a business is to ensure that people do not only see art as drawing and painting but that it can be incorporate into music glass plaques,clothes and shoes.
